Paragraphs are a distinct section of a piece of writing, usually dealing with a single theme and indicated by a new line, indentation, or numbering.

Paragraphs are used to help your reader follow the logic of your argument.

Paragraphs play an important role in writing because they provide a framework for organizing your ideas in a logical order. Using a clear structure for your paragraphs helps guide the reader through your written work.
